# Accessing Parish Email Using Outlook on the Web

This guide explains how to access a parish email account using a web browser.

## Before you begin

You should have received a welcome email at your personal email address. The welcome email will:

* include the parish email address
* include the password
* link to this guide
* explain who to contact if you need help

The welcome email will usually come from [it@parishofchingford.org.uk](mailto:it@parishofchingford.org.uk), although it may occasionally be forwarded to you by another parish office holder such as the rector or a churchwarden.

If you cannot find the welcome email, please check your spam or junk folder before requesting assistance.

### Further assistance
If you still cannot find the welcome email or you need further assistance, please contact the Parish IT Administrator at [it@parishofchingford.org.uk](mailto:it@parishofchingford.org.uk).


## Step 1 - Open Outlook

Open: <a href="https://outlook.office.com/mail/inbox" target="CPC">https://outlook.office.com/mail/inbox</a> *Opens in new tab*.

You should see the Microsoft sign-in page. Consider saving this as a bookmark so you can easily find it again later.

## Step 2 - Sign in

1. Enter your parish email address included in your welcome email
2. Click **Next**.
3. Enter the password provided to you in your welcome email.
4. Click **Sign in**.

## Step 3 - Complete any first-time prompts

Depending on how the account has been configured, Microsoft may ask you to [complete some first-time setup steps](/guides/04-first-time-microsoft-sign-in-prompts.md).

## Step 4 - Check your mailbox

Once Outlook opens in your browser you should see:

* Inbox
* Sent Items
* Drafts
* Deleted Items

Send a test email to yourself and confirm it arrives.

## Signing out

When finished or you wish to sign-out:

1. Click your profile picture/avatar in the top-right corner.
2. Click **Sign out**.
3. Close the browser window if using a public or shared computer.

## Troubleshooting

### I don't know my password

Please check the welcome email sent to your personal email address.


### Microsoft says my password is incorrect

Please check that the password has been entered exactly as shown in the welcome email sent to your personal email address.

If copying and pasting the password, make sure you have not accidentally included an extra space before or after the password.
